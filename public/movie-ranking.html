The Best Movies According to Karen

My top 3 movies of all time

Spirited Away (2001)

A magical coming-of-age story about Chihiro, a girl who must survive in a world of spirits after her parents are transformed into pigs. Widely regarded as Ghibli’s masterpiece and winner of the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature.

My Neighbor Totoro (1988)

A heartwarming tale of two sisters who move to the countryside and encounter friendly forest spirits, including the beloved Totoro. A timeless story of childhood wonder and imagination.

Howl’s Moving Castle (2004)

A whimsical yet profound story about Sophie, cursed to become an old woman, and her adventures with the mysterious wizard Howl in his magical moving castle. Themes of love, war, and self-discovery shine throughout.

Kiki’s Delivery Service (1989)

A charming film following young witch Kiki as she leaves home to find independence in a seaside city. It’s a touching story of growth, confidence, and belonging.

Ponyo (2008)

A magical retelling of The Little Mermaid, Ponyo tells the story of a goldfish princess who longs to live as a human after befriending a young boy named Sosuke. A colorful and heartwarming film about friendship, family, and nature.
